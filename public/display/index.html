HOW TO PLAY
1 · The Game
  • We surveyed 100 people. Their top answers are on the board.
  • Two teams compete to guess the most popular answers.
  • Each answer is worth points based on how many people said it.
2 · Face-Off
  • One player from each team faces off — first to buzz in answers.
  • The highest-rated answer wins control of the round.
  • The controlling team takes turns guessing the rest of the board.
3 · Strikes
  • A wrong answer = ✕ STRIKE.
  • Three strikes and the other team gets one chance to steal.
  • Steal a remaining answer → take the entire pot. Miss → original team keeps it.
4 · Winning
  • Whichever team wins the round adds the pot to their score.
  • Highest total score after all rounds wins.
  • Survey says… good luck.
